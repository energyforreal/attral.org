PCB Design

From concept schematic to production-ready Gerber files, ATTRAL designs for signal integrity, thermal performance, and DFM from day one.

Complete hardware production pipeline: fabrication, SMT & through-hole assembly, and test validation with trusted partners.

  • AOI, functional test & burn-in
